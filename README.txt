----------------------------
THEME OF GAME
----------------------------
The theme of evolution is implemented through both the gameplay and narrative of the project. The player starts as a monkey affected by the radiation of the meteor that struck. This triggers a journey of transformation. As the monkey climbs the mountain, the monkey collects bananas and finds flint and steel that represents that the monkey is a step closer to evolving. The flint and steel and fire within the game represents a leap in evolution both physically and intellectually. Once the monkey reaches the top of the mountain, this is where the evolution of the monkey is complete and the monkey has “become human.” 


----------------------------
GAME INSTRUCTIONS
----------------------------
Play as a monkey and climb up a mountain while escaping radiation.
Collect bananas to restore health and reach the peak to evolve.


Controls:
- Arrow Keys : Move
- Shift      : Run
- Up Arrow   : Jump


----------------------------
GAME WALKTHROUGH
----------------------------
1. Start at the base of the mountain.
2. Move upward by jumping between platforms.
3. Collect bananas to survive radiation damage.
4. Avoid falling off platforms (results in death).
5. Reach checkpoints (represented by flint and steel).
6. Continue climbing toward the peak.
7. Reach the top to evolve into a human (win condition).
